Q: What is the eligibility for placement?
A: Students must have no active backlogs and maintain a CGPA of 7.0 or above.

Q: What companies visit for campus placement?
A: TCS, Infosys, Capgemini, Wipro, and Deloitte are regular recruiters.

Q: How many attempts are allowed for placement?
A: Each student is allowed up to 3 attempts unless placed in a dream company.
